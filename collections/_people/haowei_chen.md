Currently a Postdoctoral Research Associate in the Systems, Cognition, and Control Laboratory at the University of Florida with Dr. Rushikesh L. Kamalapurkar, her research spans operator theory, optimal control, machine learning, quantum computing, and fractional analysis, with publications in leading journals and international conferences. She also brings interdisciplinary expertise spanning AI model development, advanced data analytics, and innovative mathematics education.
